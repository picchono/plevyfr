Yōkobo wins the Kawaii Kansei Design Award

Yōkobo, a cross term between "Yōkoso" ("welcome" in Japanese) and "robot", is a small "robject" designed and created as a result of a study conducted by the team of Gentiane Venture laboratory, Professor at Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology , in collaboration with the Orange Company, the Laboratory of Digital Sciences of Nantes (LS2N) and the Strate School of Design.
